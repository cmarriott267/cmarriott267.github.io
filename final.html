My Final Project!

The program below is a take on the classic arcade game, Space Invaders!
in this version, you use the left and right arrow keys to move your ship along the botton of the screen
and hold the up arrow key to shoot at the alien invader while avoiding incoming fire.
The shots from the invader come much faster and your shots move quite slow so timing is everything!
It only takes one shot to kill the invader so learn to lead the shots and avoid getting caught in the aliens path!
